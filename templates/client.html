Zeppelin Cloud

Yachting in the Cloud

The Zeppelin Cloud App is a vital tool for yacht management, tailored to serve not just Captains and Managers, but also owners.

Zeppelin combines the functionalities of both CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and CMS (Content Management System) frameworks, providing a comprehensive all-in-one solution.

All of this is managed within a secure database, ensuring that your information is protected while maintaining accessibility.

Financial Features:
  • Real-time Financial Data: Stay informed with up-to-the-minute financial figures.
  • Invoice Approval Workflow: Manage and approve invoices effortlessly.
  • Saved Financial Expense Reports: Stored and organized financial reports, and statements.
Operations Management:
  • Document Management: Keep vital documents secure and accessible.
  • Vessel Data: Retrieve & Export vessel details quickly and accurately.
  • Automated alerts for certificates and licenses
Crew & Guest Data:
  • View vital crew / guest information without hassle.
  • Create and manipulate vessel manifests with simplified selections.
  • Organzied and stored preference details for guests.
